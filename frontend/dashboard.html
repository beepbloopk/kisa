What Kisa Does

Every sighting builds a story.

Reports from your community stack up into a living biography of every cat in your neighbourhood. Location history, health notes, and status all come together from what people see and share.

Sighting Reports

Log where you saw a cat, attach a photo, and note how they looked. The whole process takes less than a minute.

Auto-Generated Profiles

Each cat gets a living profile built from community sightings. No one person has to manage it all.

Live Community Map

How It Works

Three simple steps.

You don't need to be a rescue volunteer. Just be someone who notices the cats around them.

Spot and Report

See a cat? Log a sighting in under a minute. Pin the location, add a photo, note how they looked.

02

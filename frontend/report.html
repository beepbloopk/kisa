How your report helps

One report. Three things happen.

Community Verified

Your sighting is logged to a shared, transparent record. Other members can confirm, correct, or add detail to what you've reported — building accuracy over time.

Builds Cat Profiles

Every report automatically contributes to the cat's living profile — adding to their location history, health timeline, and community description. No one person manages it all.

SOS Alerts for Emergencies

Marking a cat as Emergency (SOS) immediately alerts nearby volunteers and rescue groups. A rescue is coordinated without anyone having to figure it out alone.
